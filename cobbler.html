Easy Peach Cobbler Recipe with Fresh and Canned Peaches

Aug 17 2023 Time to make: 1h Author: Ulurt Nileman

Introducing our mouthwatering Peach Cobbler recipe—a delightful fusion of fresh and canned peaches, with amazing flavors and textures. With a golden, biscuit-like topping that crowns a luscious peach filling, this dessert embodies comfort and indulgence. Follow our simple steps to craft a dessert that's both inviting and heartwarming, making it a perfect treat for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups sliced fresh peaches (about 5-6 peaches) or 1 can (29 ounces) sliced peaches, drained
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Ground cinnamon (for sprinkling)
  • Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream (for serving, optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. If using fresh peaches, wash, peel, and slice them into bite-sized pieces. Place them in a mixing bowl and toss with lemon juice. If using canned peaches, drain them well and set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine ½ cup of granulated sugar and the sliced peaches. Gently stir to coat the peaches with sugar, then set aside for about 15 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, ½ cup of granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add in the milk and vanilla extract, and stir until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour the melted butter into an 8x8-inch baking dish. Pour the batter evenly over the butter. Do not stir.
  6. Evenly distribute the sugared peach slices over the batter. Avoid stirring.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cobbler is bubbling around the edges.
  8. Sprinkle ground cinnamon over the top of the cobbler once out of the oven.
  9. Allow the peach cobbler to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm on its own or with a scoop of ice cream or whipped cream.

Conclusion: Indulge in the timeless charm of homemade peach cobbler, a comforting dessert that seamlessly combines fresh and canned peaches, topped with a golden crust. A treat that's warm, inviting, and simply delightful.
