How to Make Perfect Potato Twisters at Home - A Step-by-Step Recipe Guide

Aug 26 2023 Time to make: 15 minutes Author: Alex Johnson

Craving a delicious and crispy snack that's both fun to eat and easy to make at home? Look no further than homemade potato twisters! These delightful spiral-cut potatoes on sticks are a popular street food, and now you can recreate them in your own kitchen. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of making potato twisters that are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. Get ready to impress your family and friends with this irresistible treat.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Wooden skewers or sticks
  • Cooking oil for frying
  • Salt and any desired seasonings (paprika, garlic powder, etc.)

Equipment:

  • Spiral cutter for potatoes
  • Deep fryer or a heavy-bottomed pot for frying
  • Paper towels for draining excess oil

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes

  1. Start by washing the potatoes thoroughly to remove any dirt.
  2. Peel the potatoes if desired, although leaving the skin on can add extra texture and flavor.

Step 2: Attach the Sticks

  1. Insert wooden skewers or sticks into the base of each potato, making sure they're secure. This will be the "handle" for your potato twisters.

Step 3: Spiral Cutting

  1. Using a spiral cutter designed for potatoes, carefully insert the pointed end of the skewered potato into the spiral cutter's spike.
  2. Begin turning the handle of the cutter while gently pushing the potato forward. The blade of the cutter will create perfect spiral cuts as you continue to turn.
  3. Work your way through the entire potato, creating an even spiral pattern.

Step 4: Shaping the Twisters

  1. Gently slide the cut potato down the skewer, spreading out the spirals to create a visually appealing shape.

Step 5: Preparing for Frying

  1. Heat cooking oil in a deep fryer or heavy-bottomed pot to around 350°F (175°C).
  2. Carefully lower the potato twisters into the hot oil, holding onto the wooden skewer. Fry them in batches to avoid overcrowding the fryer.
  3. Fry the twisters until they are golden brown and crispy, usually around 4-6 minutes.

Step 6: Draining and Seasoning

  1. Once fried, use tongs to lift the twisters out of the oil and let them drain on a plate lined with paper towels.
  2. While the twisters are still hot, season them with salt and any additional desired seasonings. Traditional choices include paprika, garlic powder, or a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Step 7: Serving and Enjoying

  1. Arrange your homemade potato twisters on a serving platter or in a paper cone for a street food vibe.
  2. Serve them hot and crispy, and don't forget to provide some dipping sauces for extra flavor!

Making potato twisters at home is not only a fun cooking project but also a surefire way to create a crowd-pleasing snack. By following this step-by-step guide, you'll be able to whip up your very own batch of delicious potato twisters that rival the ones you find at food trucks or fairs. Whether you're hosting a gathering or simply treating yourself, these crispy and flavorful potato twisters are bound to be a hit. Enjoy the satisfaction of creating a beloved snack from scratch and savor every crispy bite!
