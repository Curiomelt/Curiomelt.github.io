How to Make Perfect Naan Bread in the Air Fryer: Easy Homemade Recipe

Aug 10th 2023 Time to make: 40min Author: Ethan Davis

Craving the delightful flavors of Indian cuisine? Learn how to make delicious naan bread right in your air fryer with this simple recipe. In just a few easy steps, you'll have fluffy, flavorful naan that's perfect for pairing with your favorite curries or even creating naan pizza! Let's dive into the world of homemade naan with the convenience of your air fryer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: minced garlic, chopped cilantro for topping

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Naan Dough:

In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Allow it to sit for about 5 minutes, until it becomes frothy. In a larger mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture, plain yogurt, and olive oil. Mix until a soft dough forms.

2. Knead and Rest:

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rest for about an hour, or until it doubles in size.

3. Preheat the Air Fryer:

Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C) for a few minutes.

4. Shape the Naan:

Divide the rested dough into smaller portions, typically around 6 pieces. Roll each piece into an oval or round shape, approximately 1/4-inch thick.

5. Cook in the Air Fryer:

Carefully place the shaped naan bread in the preheated air fryer basket, making sure not to overcrowd. Cook for 2-3 minutes on one side, then flip the naan and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Keep an eye on them; they should puff up and develop some golden brown spots.

6. Optional Toppings:

If you'd like to add extra flavor, you can brush the cooked naan with melted butter and sprinkle minced garlic and chopped cilantro on top.

7. Serve and Enjoy:

Your homemade naan bread is ready! Serve it warm with your favorite Indian dishes or use it as a base for naan pizza in the air fryer. It's a versatile treat that's sure to delight your taste buds.

Tips:

  • For naan pizza, top your cooked naan with tomato sauce, cheese, and your preferred toppings. Place it back in the air fryer for a few minutes until the cheese melts.
  • To reheat naan, simply place it in the air fryer for about 1 minute to restore its freshness.

Conclusion:

Now you know how to make authentic naan bread in the air fryer! This easy recipe lets you enjoy the rich flavors of Indian cuisine at home. From classic naan to creative naan pizza, your air fryer can do it all. Try this recipe today and elevate your meals with homemade naan bread that's sure to impress.
