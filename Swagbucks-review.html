Is Swagbucks Legit, Safe, and Worth Your Time?

Nov 27th 2023 7 Min read Author: Joe Bartolozzi

Looking for ways to earn some extra bucks without breaking a sweat? You might have stumbled upon Swagbucks for easy money-making opportunities online. But before you even try out Swagbucks, you probably are wondering if making money online through the site is really that simple, and that Swagbucks is even legit, safe, and worth your precious time? Can you really earn real money from it? And where can you use those Swagbucks you've earned? Fear not, for this comprehensive guide will address all your queries and ensure you're well informed to make a good decision if trying out Swagbucks is actually worth it, or not.

What Even is Swagbucks?

Swagbucks, is a "get-paid-to" (GPT) platform, has gained widespread recognition for providing users with opportunities to earn rewards through various online activities. Swagbucks lets you earn free gift cards and cash. But how does it work? Essentially, you can earn points, known as Swagbucks (SB), by taking paid online surveys, playing games, surfing the web, watching videos, shopping online, and more. It's a simple and straightforward way to monetize your online activities.The legitimacy of Swagbucks is acknowledged by its long-standing presence in the online rewards landscape and positive reviews from users. Swagbucks was actually launched way back in 2008, and is roughly 15 years old now in 2023. Swagbucks was founded by Scott Dudelson, Josef Gorowitz, and Eron Zehavi.

Image credits: https://www.swagbucks.com

How Does Swagbucks Themself Make Money?

But how does Swagbucks themself make money, after basically handing out free money to millions of their users. Well that is actually a little tricky to explain, but I keep it short and simple. So basically Swagbucks operates on a multifaceted revenue model, primarily deriving income through affiliate commissions when its members make purchases from an extensive network of over 1,500 eligible partners. Beyond this, the company strategically leverages another revenue stream by offering valuable consumer demographic data to retailers.

Is Swagbucks Legit and Safe?

Is Swagbucks Legit?

Numerous reviews and websites is for the most part checking for the legitimacy of Swagbucks. The platform has paid out over $650,000,000 to its members, with countless users earning substantial amounts.

Swagbucks have actually also been rated at almost 100% legitimacy rating and a solid A standing with the "Better Business Bureau", showcasing that Swagbucks is a genuine platform. Users worldwide have mostly confirmed to its legitimacy, making it a reliable option for those looking to make a little extra cash. But of course many users have also experienced some weird glitches on Swagbucks, like not getting paid after completing a survey or not being able to log in to their account. But these problems could also just be technical difficulties, but it is still nice to know that not everything with these money making platforms is 100% flawless.

Is Swagbucks Safe?

Swagbucks has established itself as a safe platform. With its robust payment system and a track record of secure transactions, users can (for the most part) rest assured that their efforts won't go unrewarded or compromised. Of Course this doesn't mean that you should give your 100% trust in it, and you should always be careful in not falling for any scams or some sort that wants your sensitive data.

Is Swagbucks Surveys Safe?

Taking surveys on Swagbucks is generally safe, with the platform implementing measures to protect user information. However, it's always advisable to be cautious and provide information selectively.

Is Swagbucks Worth Your Time?

Earning Potential

Swagbucks provides diverse opportunities to earn, including surveys, watching videos, and playing games. The more activities you engage in, the higher your earning potential. But it could pretty much vary if completing a survey for example, would actually be worth your time. Some surveys can span to be completed in just 10 minutes, or others may take 45 minutes. But in general you would most likely be done doing a survey in at least 20 or 25 minutes (depending on the type of survey), or other activities on the site.

Image by upklyak

Now if that amount of time is worth actually getting the earnings from Swagbucks, then it could definitely vary. But in general you could earn from $5, to $100 after completing an activity according to the Swagbucks website. Some reviews point out that people have reportedly said to get decently rich from the site, while others claim it isn’t worth your time, and that you would earn more getting a real job instead. But this ultimately comes down on what you think. If you just want a quick and easy way to earn some small amounts of money, as a side hustle, then go for it! But if you are actually trying to get rich from the site, then you might have to grind really hard to achieve this goal.

Flexibility

One of Swagbucks' strengths is its flexibility. Users can participate in activities at their convenience, making it an excellent option for those with busy schedules. It is especially a really great option if you have some spare time on your hands. Like who doesn’t want to earn some extra cash from completing some easy surveys or playing a game. Of Course some activities from Swagbucks can be a little more longer than others, so you have to check beforehand if you even have the time to complete those activities, or if it is even worth completing. For example if you use 45 minutes to complete a survey that is only worth 4$.

Rewards Variety

Swagbucks offers a plethora of reward options, from gift cards to PayPal cash. The variety ensures that users can choose rewards that align with their preferences. The Swagbucks website claims that you only need 1$ worth of Swagbucks points (SB), which is roughly 100 SB. You can also trade those points to redeem different kinds of gift cards, like for example Amazon gift cards, Steam gift cards and Zalando gift cards.

Time Efficiency

While the earning potential is there, it's essential to consider the time invested. Evaluate whether the rewards justify the time spent on various activities. Because some activities could be a little too long, and “pointless” if you would call it. Because you could earn very little, even if you have used a lot of time and effort into it. So just keep that in mind when trying to choose an activity on swagbucks.
