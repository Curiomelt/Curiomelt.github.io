The Top 10 Best Anime Series to Watch in 2023

mar 19th 2023 4 Min read Author: Ulurt Nileman

Anime enthusiasts, get ready for a thrilling year ahead! As we dive deeper into the captivating realm of anime, 2023 promises to be an extraordinary year filled with a diverse array of genres and enthralling storytelling. From highly anticipated new seasons of beloved series to intriguing new shows on the horizon, there's an abundance of anime to look forward to. In this article you would find the top 10 anime series that you must watch in 2023.

1. "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba - Swordsmith Village Arc"

Prepare to be swept away by the storm that is "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba." This phenomenal series has captivated audiences worldwide with its gripping tale of Tanjiro Kamado, a young demon slayer on a quest for vengeance and a cure for his demonized sister. In the third season, titled "Swordsmith Village Arc," Tanjiro and his friends venture into a mysterious village renowned for its legendary swordsmiths. Brace yourself for stunning animation, intense action sequences, and emotionally charged storytelling in one of the most highly anticipated anime releases of 2023.

2. "Attack on Titan" Season 4 Part 3

The epic saga of "Attack on Titan" reaches its climactic conclusion in the highly awaited third part of Season 4. Prepare for an intense narrative as the Survey Corps confronts new adversaries and unravels long-held secrets. With jaw-dropping action, shocking revelations, and heartfelt moments, this anime masterpiece is sure to leave a lasting impression. Immerse yourself in impeccable animation and an immersive storyline that has enthralled fans worldwide, making "Attack on Titan" Season 4 Part 3 an unmissable entry on your watchlist.

3. "My Hero Academia Season 6"

Join the journey of Izuku Midoriya and his friends as they face fresh challenges in "My Hero Academia" Season 6. Set in a world where almost everyone possesses extraordinary powers known as Quirks, this modern shonen classic continues to captivate audiences with its exhilarating action and themes of heroism and friendship. Experience the awe-inspiring animation, compelling characters, and inspiring storylines that have made "My Hero Academia" a beloved series among fans worldwide.

4. "Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2"

Delve into the dark and supernatural world of "Jujutsu Kaisen" Season 2, a series that has skyrocketed in popularity. Join Yuji Itadori and his companions as they combat evil curses while unraveling the mysteries of the Jujutsu realm. With its stellar animation, memorable characters, and thrilling battles, this unique blend of supernatural horror, action, and comedy promises an enthralling experience for fans eager to witness the next chapter of this captivating story.

5. "One Punch Man Season 3"

Prepare for another round of satirical humor and awe-inspiring action as "One Punch Man" returns with Season 3. Follow the invincible Saitama on his quest for a worthy opponent as he faces new challenges and encounters formidable foes. Brace yourself for the absurdly powerful heroics, dazzling animation, and over-the-top adventures that have made this series an absolute fan-favorite.

6. "The Rising of the Shield Hero Season 3"

Watch the captivating isekai tale of Naofumi Iwatani in "The Rising of the Shield Hero" Season 3. Witness Naofumi's relentless pursuit to defeat calamitous waves and clear his tarnished name. With its unique take on the isekai genre, intriguing character dynamics, and immersive world-building, this series promises an engaging and thrilling adventure that fans of the series won't want to miss.

7. "Haikyuu!!: To The Top Part 2"

Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled journey as the Karasuno High School volleyball team continues its pursuit of victory in "Haikyuu!!: To The Top Part 2." Brace yourself for intense sports action, heartfelt character relationships, and the never-ending spirit of teamwork. Experience the dynamic animation and relatable characters that have made "Haikyuu!!" a beloved sports anime.

8. "Beastars Season 3"

Enter the thought-provoking world of "Beastars" Season 3, where anthropomorphic animals navigate complex relationships between herbivores and carnivores. Follow the gray wolf Legoshi as he grapples with his emotions and battles prejudice in a divided society. Witness the stunning animation, deep character development, and profound social commentary that have made "Beastars" an intriguing and unique addition to the anime landscape.

9. "Black Clover Season 5"

"Black Clover" has been a popular fantasy anime that follows the story of Asta, a young boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything. Despite his limitations, Asta dreams of becoming the Wizard King, the most powerful magician in the kingdom. In the fifth season, Asta and his friends continue their journey to become stronger and protect their kingdom from evil forces. Known for its fast-paced action, colorful characters, and magical world-building, "Black Clover Season 5" is eagerly anticipated by fans who are excited to see Asta's growth as a mage.

10. "Mob Psycho 100 Season 3"

Witness the captivating psychic adventures of Shigeo Kageyama, also known as Mob, in "Mob Psycho 100" Season 3. Traverse Mob's incredible powers, intricate relationships, and newfound challenges as he faces formidable psychic adversaries. Immerse yourself in the unique animation style, complex character dynamics, and the perfect blend of humor and action that have garnered critical acclaim for this series.

In conclusion, 2023 is shaping up to be an exciting year for anime lovers with a diverse range of anime series set to be released. From action-packed adventures to heartwarming dramas, there is something for everyone to look forward to. With the return of fan-favorite shows like "Demon Slayer," "One Punch Man," and "The Rising of the Shield Hero," as well as the continuation of beloved sports anime like "Haikyuu!!," anime enthusiasts have much to anticipate.
