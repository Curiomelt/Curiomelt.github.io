Sonic Origin and Sonic Origin Plus Game Review

Nov 11th 2023 7 Min read Author: Adam Jones

Sonic the Hedgehog has come a long way since the first ever Sonic game in 1991 for the Sega Genesis, and since then Sega have just non stop put more love and effort into their Sonic games. And now we can experience some of that early history of sonic games all again. If you're a die-hard fan or just someone looking to try a new Sonic game, then look no further. In this comprehensive review, we'll explore the ins and outs of Sonic Origins, from gameplay and release dates to platforms and characters.

Sonic Origins: A Glance at the Basics

Before we zoom into the details, let's make sure everyone's on the same page. Sonic Origins is not just a game; it's an all-in-one of Sonic's history. From classic Sonic titles to the latest and greatest adventures, this collection brings the entire Sonic experience to a new level. With revamped graphics, enhanced gameplay, and a blast from the past, Sonic Origins is set to capture the hearts of both veteran players and newcomers.

What's Inside Sonic Origins?

  • Classic Sonic Titles - Experience the nostalgia with iconic titles like Sonic the Hedgehog, Sonic the Hedgehog 2, Sonic CD, and more! Sonic Origins packs a punch with a comprehensive collection that spans the evolution of Sonic over the years.
  • Revamped Graphics for a Modern Twist - Experience the magic of Sonic like never before with updated visuals that breathe new life into your favorite Hedgehog. The developers haven't just remastered; they've revitalized Sonic's adventures for a modern gaming experience.
  • Unlockable Content and Bonuses - Sonic Origins Plus? Yes, you heard it right! Get ready for exclusive content and bonuses that come with the Sonic Origins Plus edition. We'll uncover more about this exciting addition later in the article.

Image: Intro animation from Sonic CD

Sonic Origins Release Date

Sonic Origins has already been released on the 23rd of June 2022. Since then, many gaming YouTubers have reviewed the game, and some loved it while others disliked it. Since then, Sonic Origins has had some bug fixes, enemy updates, and it even managed to be released for the PS5 console.

Sonic Origins on Multiple Platforms

Sonic Origins on Nintendo Switch

For the gamers on the go, Sonic Origins is, of course, available on the Nintendo Switch. Imagine the thrill of experiencing Sonic's lightning-fast speed on a portable device! Sonic Origins on the Nintendo Switch is the perfect companion for your on-the-move trips.

Image: Gameplay from Sonic CD, played on a Nintendo Switch

Sonic Origins on PS4

Sonic Origins is also available for the PS4. It's Sonic like you've never seen before, with all the action right at your fingertips.

Sonic Origins on Steam

For the PC gaming enthusiasts, Sonic Origins will be hitting Steam. Play and experience Sonic's universe with the flexibility and power of your gaming rig. Sonic Origins on Steam ensures that the Hedgehog's adventures are not limited to a specific console but are accessible to all PC gamers worldwide.

Image: Gameplay from Sonic The Hedgehog 3 & Knuckles, played on a PC

Sonic Origins Plus

So, what's the deal with Sonic Origins Plus? This enhanced edition promises an even more thrilling Sonic experience, with additional content and features. Let's break it down:

  • Exclusive Games and DLCs - Sonic Origins Plus comes packed with exclusive games and downloadable content that takes the Sonic adventure to new heights. It's not just a collection.
  • Physical Edition for Collectors - Are you a Sonic memorabilia collector? Sonic Origins Plus offers a physical edition for those who love to have a tangible piece of Sonic history. Limited edition, unlimited excitement!
  • Enhanced Multiplayer Options - Is Sonic Origins multiplayer? Absolutely! Sonic Origins Plus brings enhanced multiplayer options, allowing you to share the Sonic experience with friends and family. It's not just a solo adventure.

Sonic Origins: FAQs Answered!

Q1: Is Sonic Origins multiplayer?

Yes, indeed! Sonic Origins Plus introduces enhanced multiplayer options, making it the perfect game for shared gameplay with friends and family.

Q2: How many copies have Sonic Origin sold?

It's not clear. But some sources point out that Sonic Origin may have sold more than 750,000 copies around Steam and consoles like Nintendo, PS4, Xbox, and PS5.

Q3: What platforms will Sonic Origins be available on?

Sonic Origins is available on Nintendo Switch, PS4 and PS5, Xbox, and Steam. Whether you're a console enthusiast or a PC gamer, you can still enjoy the game either way.

Conclusion

As we wrap up our journey through the Sonic universe, one thing is clear – Sonic Origins is not just a game; it's a celebration of Sonic's legacy. With a mix of classic titles, modern graphics, and exclusive content, Sonic Origins promises an adventure like no other.
